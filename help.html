Help & Support

How to navigate and use every page on this site

01

Navigating the Site

Use the menu at the top of every page — Home, Gallery, Booking, Help, About — to move between sections. The current page is underlined in the menu so you always know where you are.

02

Viewing the Gallery

On the Gallery page, hover over any photo to see it zoom slightly. Below the photos, the table lists trip duration, group size, and price for each destination — scroll sideways on smaller screens if the table doesn't fit.

03

Filling Out the Booking Form

On the Booking page, fill in your name, email, and phone number, then choose your major and destination from the dropdowns. Select your gender with a radio button, and check any hobbies that interest you. Fields marked as required must be completed before the form can be submitted.

04

Understanding Images

Every image on this site includes descriptive alt text, so screen readers can describe the picture even if it doesn't load. If an image looks broken, check your internet connection — images are loaded from an external source.

05

Contacting Us

For any question not answered here, visit the About page for contact details, or leave a note in the Comments box on the Booking form.
